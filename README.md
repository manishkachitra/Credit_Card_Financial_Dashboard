# 💳 Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing credit card customer and transaction data to uncover business trends, customer behavior, revenue drivers, and operational performance. The solution integrates PostgreSQL and Power BI to create interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights into customer demographics, card usage patterns, transaction trends, and revenue generation.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify high-value customer segments, and support data-driven decision-making through visual analytics.

---

##  Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to develop a comprehensive Credit Card Financial Dashboard that:

- Monitors key financial and operational KPIs.
- Tracks customer demographics and spending behavior.
- Identifies revenue-generating customer segments.
- Analyzes transaction patterns and card performance.
- Provides actionable insights to improve customer engagement and profitability.

#  Key Insights

### Customer Insights

- Male customers generated approximately **31M revenue**, compared to **26M revenue** from female customers.
- Customers aged **40–60 years** contributed the highest revenue share.
- High-income customers generated significantly higher revenue than medium and low-income groups.
- Married customers contributed more revenue than single customers.
- Texas, New York, and California were the top revenue-generating states.

### Transaction Insights

- Blue Card customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Blue and Silver card categories contributed over **90% of total business revenue and transactions**.
- Swipe transactions dominated customer payment behavior.
- Bills, Entertainment, and Fuel were the highest spending categories.
- Revenue and transaction volumes increased during Q4, indicating stronger year-end activity.

#  Business Recommendations

### Customer Strategy

- Introduce loyalty and reward programs for high-value customers.
- Develop retention campaigns targeting medium-income customer segments.
- Increase engagement initiatives for younger customers.

### Card Portfolio Strategy

- Strengthen Blue Card customer retention initiatives.
- Promote Gold and Platinum card upgrades to increase customer lifetime value.
- Design premium reward programs for high-spending customers.

### Transaction Strategy

- Encourage online and digital payments through cashback and rewards.
- Create targeted offers for fuel, entertainment, and bill-payment categories.
- Improve customer adoption of digital transaction channels.

### Regional Strategy

- Expand marketing efforts in top-performing states such as Texas, New York, and California.
- Investigate opportunities for growth in lower-performing regions.
